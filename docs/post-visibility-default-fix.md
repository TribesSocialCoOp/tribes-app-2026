# 🔧 Check Your Tribe Visibility Settings

Hey founders, quick heads up on a setting that may have tripped you up.

**Previously, new tribes defaulted to Private (invite-only).** This meant your tribe wasn't showing up in Discover, and nobody could find or join it unless you sent them a direct link. We've heard from a few of you wondering why your tribe wasn't getting any traction, and this was likely why.

**We've fixed this.** New tribes now default to **Public** so they're discoverable from day one. If you *want* a private tribe, you can still toggle it. It's just an intentional choice now instead of an easy-to-miss default.

## What to do

If you created a tribe and it's been quiet, head to your **Tribe Settings** and check the **Visibility** toggle. If it says 🔒 Private and you didn't mean for it to be, flip it to 🌐 Public and you should start seeing people find you.

Thanks for building with us 🤙
